About the Application

PRGI Press automates title verification, registration and cataloguing for more than 160 k+ book records. It replaces manual spreadsheet look-ups with a fast phonetic-search engine powered by Supabase.

How Title Verification Works

When you enter a proposed title, the engine applies several layers of checks to minimise duplicates and conflicts:

  1. Phonetic Matching – converts titles to Soundex/Metaphone keys to catch homophones (“Knight/ Night”).
  2. Spelling-Variant Expansion – normalises British↔American spellings (“colour/ color”), diacritics (“Café/ Cafe”) and common typos.
  3. Semantic Similarity – embeds each title with a transformer model; cosine similarity > 0.85 is flagged as Possible Duplicate.
  4. Restricted Words & Affixes – blocks titles containing banned words (defamatory, discriminatory, trademarked) or prohibited affixes (e.g. -pedia, Master-) unless you have clearance.
Tip: Even one flagged rule can stall registration. Resolve warnings or request an override from Editorial before resubmitting.

Typical Workflow

  1. Open Title Verification.
  2. Enter the proposed title and click Verify.
  3. If the result is Accepted, continue to Register Title; otherwise review the conflicts.
  4. After registering, our Officials will approve the registration, once all other external processes are completed.
  5. On successful registration the title status appears instantly in History › Registration as Registered.
